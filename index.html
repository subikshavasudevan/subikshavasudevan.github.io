Our Society

About SNS IMUN

SNS International Model United Nations Society is a student-driven organization operating across SNS Institutions. The society provides a structured environment where students explore international relations, diplomacy, and global policy discussions through Model United Nationsâ€“based activities.

The society is committed to fostering professionalism, respect for diversity, ethical conduct, and collaborative leadership. SNS IMUN aims to equip its members with communication, negotiation, and critical-thinking skills while promoting awareness of global issues.

Membership is open to undergraduate and postgraduate students across SNS Institutions who are interested in diplomacy, global affairs, and leadership development.

Engagement Opportunities

Committees & Portfolios

SNS IMUN activities typically involve participation across various committees and portfolios, allowing members to engage based on their interests and strengths.

UNSC

United Nations Security Council

Focuses on international peace and security, addressing global conflicts, crisis situations, and strategies for maintaining stability among nations.

UNGA

United Nations General Assembly

Serves as a forum for multilateral discussion on global political, economic, social, and humanitarian issues affecting the international community.

UNHRC

United Nations Human Rights Council

Addresses global human rights concerns and promotes the protection of fundamental freedoms through discussion, resolution drafting, and advocacy.

International Press

IP

Responsible for reporting, analysis, and documentation of committee proceedings, capturing developments and perspectives during SNS IMUN activities.

Photography

Visual Documentation

Handles visual documentation and media coverage, capturing key moments, sessions, and events associated with SNS IMUN activities.
